# 📘 SECTION: WHAT IS PPC (THE PRODUCT SENSE CONTEXT)

## PPC, Done with Product Sense

Most agencies treat PPC as a traffic-generation tool. We treat it as a product feature designed to solve a specific job-to-be-done: **Acquiring high-value customers at a profitable margin.**

It’s not about bidding on keywords. It’s about understanding user intent, mapping out the buyer's journey, and delivering the right message exactly when they need it most.

Done right, PPC becomes:

- A tap you can turn on to scale revenue predictably  
- A highly measurable Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) engine  
- A feedback loop for product and market fit  
- A way to capture demand your competitors are missing  

If your ads aren't converting, it's not the algorithm's fault. It's a disconnect between user intent, messaging, and the landing page experience.

We bridge that gap.
